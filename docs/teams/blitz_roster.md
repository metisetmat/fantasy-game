# BLITZ Roster

BLITZ is the vertical benchmark roster for V0.1. It uses the official nine visible attributes from `docs/gameplay/player_attributes.md` and feeds the same derived-attribute and player-state pipeline as CONTROL.

Raw visible attribute total: 6610.

This roster is intentionally fast, creative, and foot-play capable, with lower composure and weaker structural reliability than CONTROL. BLITZ should create danger through long-play line-breaking, aggressive pressing, and chaotic finishing rather than stable possession control.

## Tactical Identity

- Tempo Half: line-breaking distributor who accepts unstable windows.
- Hook Link: fast connector who can receive vertically but is less stable under pressure than CONTROL's Hook Link.
- Forward Leader: power screen and second-wave anchor for explosive attacks.
- Goalkeeper / Free Safety: aggressive depth protector with elite Foot Play for long distribution.
- Mobile Lock: fast emergency defender and pressure hunter.
- Space Hunter: primary depth runner and chaotic finisher.
- Playmaker: high-creativity unlocker with strong Foot Play and Ball Carrying.
- Pivot: central balance player, less conservative than CONTROL's Pivot.
- Left Piston: vertical flank runner and open-side projection option.
- Right Piston: vertical flank runner and open-side projection option.

## Role Behavior Overrides

BLITZ does not use CONTROL's generic role behavior when its philosophy demands a different expression.

- Tempo Half: primary vertical trigger; looks for depth runners early; prefers line-breaking release over slow support; accepts contested lanes.
- Hook Link: contact connector; keeps chaotic phases alive; supports second balls instead of always securing safely.
- Forward Leader: collision launcher; screens long-play receivers; attacks second contact; accepts weaker rest-defense discipline.
- Goalkeeper / Free Safety: aggressive sweeper-kicker; strong long distribution; accepts more risk outside the defensive goal area.
- Mobile Lock: aggressive recovery defender; counterpresses forward; can overcommit.
- Space Hunter: depth attacker; accepts isolation; attacks loose balls and rebounds; turns chaos into scoring pressure.
- Playmaker: high-risk creator; tries direct goal, drop, and line-breaking actions; accepts turnover risk.
- Pivot: transition relay; supports vertical continuation; less positionally stable than CONTROL's Pivot.
- Left Piston: high-speed width/depth runner; attacks open-side lanes; less conservative in rest defense.
- Right Piston: high-speed width/depth runner; attacks open-side lanes; less conservative in rest defense.
